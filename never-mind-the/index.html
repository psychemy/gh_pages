Never Mind the...

Inspired by the seminal 1977 punk album by the 'Sex Pistols'. The late Queen Elizabeth II's profile is referenced from the 'Royal Mail' stamps issued from 1977-1981. The gun a Smith and Wesson .38 M5 revolver was used by plain clothes officers of The Diplomatic Protection Group (ordinary police officers weren't allowed to carry guns). These were loaded with six bullets and they carried a clip of six spare rounds. God save the Queen.

Paper-cut artworks are available as 'limited edition originals' hand cut by myself using naturally textured 45% cotton rag paper which is both light-fast and acid-free in rich deep dyed colours. They come framed in white or black. All frames are conservation standard with 40% UV protection.
